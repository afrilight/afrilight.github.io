Afrilight Consulting Services

Afrilight Consulting Services is a privately owned services company, offering professional, transparent assistance to foreign investors and trading companies in our target countries in Francophone West Africa.

We operate in the following countries:

  • Mali
  • Burkina Faso
  • Guinea
  • Côte d’Ivoire

Our experience includes solar power plants in a B to G configuration, installation of photovoltaic solutions in factories and major buildings (in a B to B configuration) and for some customers we can provide domestic smart solutions


The common barrier to optimal performance in this location is the frustrating bureaucracy and cultural nuances that, if mishandled, can determine whether an operation is successful or not.

With many years of government and private sector experience in investment and trade in West Africa, we have the relevant understanding to help foreign companies navigate the challenging local business environment and optimise their project’s efficiency.
